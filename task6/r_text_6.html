A.F. Gros-Beaune-1er Cru Les Montrevenots Blancs 2018 White Burgundy 675 Domaine A.F Gros is an estate located in Pommard, Burgundy, which was founded by Anne-Francoise Gros in 1988. Francois Parent originally from Pommard, is Anne-Francoise’s husband. He has been the winemaker of the Domaine’s wines since its creation. The know-how and reputation are recognized worldwide and its wines are perfectly expressing Pinot Noir, a unique combination of elegance and depth. Today, two of their three children work on the estate, Caroline and Mathias. The brother and sister share the wine making, vineyard management, development and marking of the Domaine.
